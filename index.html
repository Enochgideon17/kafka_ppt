Core Concepts

Producer

Applications that generate and send data (events) to Kafka.

Topics

Categories or folders where events are stored (e.g., 'orders', 'payments').

Consumer

Applications that subscribe to topics to read and process the events.

Summary & Conclusion

  • ✅ Enables high-throughput, real-time systems
  • ✅ Highly scalable with Partitions & Consumer Groups
  • ✅ Data persistence sets it apart from traditional brokers
  • ✅ Essential skill for modern microservice architectures
